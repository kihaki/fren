Why Fren?

⚡

One Click

Toggle settings instantly. No digging through Android menus or remembering ADB commands.

🔮

Liquid Glass

Native macOS 26 Liquid Glass design. Looks and feels like it belongs on your desktop.

📡

Live State

Toggles reflect actual device state in real-time. Always know what's on and what's off.

🪶

Tiny Footprint

No dock icon. Lives in your menu bar. A floating widget you can park anywhere.

🔌

Auto-Detect

Works with physical devices and emulators. Dims gracefully when nothing is connected.

🛠️

Developer-First

Show taps, layout bounds, stay awake — the dev toggles you reach for every day.

All Controls

Everything you need, nothing you don't.
